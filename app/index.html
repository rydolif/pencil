What awaits you on the course

We have developed detailed and truly interesting lessons for you, which contain a large amount of theory and practice.

You will draw not only fruits and vegetables, but also learn to convey the surface of metal, stone and glass, the texture of skin and wool.

A separate block of lessons will be devoted to drawing animals, insects and birds.

The course contains 25 lessons, each lasting from 1.5 to 3 hours. At the end of each lesson, you receive a homework assignment that must be completed and sent to the teacher for checking.

At the end of the training, you will have 20 completed, unique works that touch on various topics of visual art.

Topics that we will analyze

  • Materials and tools necessary for work;

  • Selection of colored pencils, analysis of brands and companies;

  • Technique of strokes and dense coloring;

  • Paper for graphics. How its color and texture affect the result;

  • Colourology – light and color in a picture;

  • Itenna's color circle;

  • Warm. cold colors and color combinations;

  • Aerial and linear perspective;

  • Depth in work and tonal contrast;

  • Creation of textures. Features and techniques;

  • Transfer of material and surfaces of objects;

  • Images and portraits of animals and birds;

  • Drawing various types of wool;

  • The secret of drawing an expressive look;

  • Correct arrangement of accents.

Contents of lessons 1 part

Lesson 1

Properties of light and color. Let's consider the color circle of Johannes Itenna. Let's find out what warm and cold colors are and make a color map of your set of colored pencils.

Lesson 2

Hatching technique. Let's try to create tonal transitions using different types of hatching - draw a blue layer illuminated on one side.

Lesson 3

The basics of chiaroscuro. We will work out the principles of chiaroscuro drawing using the example of drawing a green apple.

Lesson 4

An image of a banana. Let's draw a ripe banana and explain how to show the texture of the inner side of the peel with the help of an electric eraser.

Lesson 5

Drawing a pumpkin. Let's consider how a chiaroscuro picture changes as the shape of the object changes.

Lesson 6

Image of textured stones. We will show how to most realistically convey a rough stone surface.

Lesson 7

Drawing of a glass vessel. We will study the technique of drawing red transparent glass.

Lesson 8

Sketching of a copper teapot. We will learn to convey the texture of metal and realistically show the surface of a copper teapot.

Lesson 9

Image of a semi-precious stone. Tell us how to convey the shape of crystal faces and show its depth.

Lesson 10

Drawing a sea wave. Let's learn how to convey dynamics in a drawing, as well as show the shape of the wave and its surface.

Lesson 11

Let's learn how to draw a tiger's eye and convey the expressiveness of the animal's gaze. Let's draw the details of the drawing. Let's practice the technique of drawing animal fur.

Lesson 12

We will draw a person’s eye and tell you how to use pencils to convey the expressiveness of the look and show the texture of the skin. We will select colors to convey the shades of human skin.

Lesson 13

Drawing a reptile's eye - part 1. Let's study alternative methods of transferring an image to the surface of paper. Let's lay out the main tonal spots.

Lesson 14

Drawing the reptile's eye - part 2. Let's work out the details and draw the scales. Let's try to convey the expressiveness of the reptile's gaze.

Questions and Answers

What tools will be needed for training?

1 To work, you will need a set of colored pencils on an oil or wax basis (at least 48 pieces in a set).

Brands of pencils that will definitely fit:

  • KOH-I-NOOR "Polycolor" – 72 colors.
  • DERWENT "Procolour", "Colorsoft" – 72 colors.
  • FABER-CASTELL "Polychromos" – 60 colors.
  • LYRA Rembrandt "Polycolor" – 36 colors.
  • Prismacolor Premier – 72-150 colors.

The most budget option is Brutfuner pencils - 160 colors

2 Any smooth graphic paper. A4 format, white color, density not less than 180 grams. You can purchase Canson Iris Vivaldi paper (240 g) individually at an artist store. As a last resort, use regular drawing paper.

3 Simple pencil (2H, for example).

4 White and black water-based paint. (acrylic or gouache).

5 Brush with a sharp tip (synthetic No. 4, for example).

6 Regular and electric eraser for colored pencils.

What is the total cost of all the tools needed for training?

From 2500 depending on your budget.

Are watercolor or pastel pencils suitable?

Watercolors are suitable for our program, but we must take into account that they are harder than wax or oil-based pencils, so drawings with such pencils may turn out less bright.

Pastels, unfortunately, will not work.

Do you need an easel for drawing with pencils?

No easel needed. For normal, comfortable work you need a comfortable chair, table and a well-lit workplace.
